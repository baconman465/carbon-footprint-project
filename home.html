Saving Power
An easy way to reduce your power usage is to replace incandescent lightbulbs with LED lightbulbs. LED lightbulbs use 85 percent less energy and last 25 times longer, making this a much better option. You could also stream media through your TV instead of a gaming console, as it uses 10 times less energy.
Reducing Utilities
It does involve giving up some comforts, but reducing the amount of utilities you use can be effective. Some good ways to accomplish this are to turn down the heat, not setting you fridge temperature below what is needed and turning down your water heater, 120 degrees Fahrenheit should be good.
